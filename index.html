About Me


Ever since I was little, I was interested in how parts functioned and how they can interconnect with others to form a larger moving piece.
My grandfather played a large role of influencing my interest and encouraging me to study mechanical engineering.
Now, I am student majoring in mechanical engineering and will be expected to receive my degree during the spring of May 2022.
I study mechanical engineer with the goal of participating in projects that will potentially impact the world currently or futuristically.
As a mechanical engineer, I aspire to build personal character, discover my own engineering path, and building a mastery of my craft.
