Standard Valve Assesmbly

The computer-aided-design drawings of a standard valve assembly.

During the fall semester of 2021 for my Computer-Aided Design course, I was tasked with the objective of creating a standard valve assembly consisting of 17 different parts. Each part was drawn and the whole valve was assembled using Creo. Each part on the assembly drawing was ballooned and a bill of materials was created that correlates to the part.
